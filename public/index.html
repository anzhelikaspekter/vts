  • 54%

    Cryptocurrency Mining: Stability and Transparency

    Cryptocurrency mining is our core business and we consider it one of the most stable and reliable businesses in the world of cryptocurrencies. Unlike trading, which is subject to high risks and fluctuations, mining allows for a stable and predictable income. We have created all the necessary conditions for efficient and comfortable mining, which makes it the main source of our profit - 54% of our total income.

    Our experience in mining started in 2019, when we installed the first 10,000 miners, and since then we have continued to actively develop this business. We provide our partners with full control and transparency of processes: everyone can monitor the activity of our mining farm in real time.

  • 10%

    Selling Heat from Mining: New Income Source

    Not many people know, but the excess heat generated by mining farms can be used for commercial purposes. While most farmers use this heat for space heating and some don't even pay attention to it, we have found a way to turn excess heat into an additional source of income.

    With the help of our technologists, we have developed a business model that allows us to sell the heat generated by the miners. This line of business already brings us up to 10% profit from the total turnover of mining assets.

    How does it work?

    If we consume 50 MW of electricity (corresponding to about 1,000 miners), the excess heat of 5 MW can be effectively utilised for sale. We sell this heat at market electricity prices, making a profit of up to 10% of turnover. This is an area of active development and we plan to expand it further to maximise all opportunities to increase profitability and improve our financial stability.

  • 5%

    Investing in metals: stability and asset protection

    While metals investments don’t yield as high returns as other areas, they are crucial for our asset preservation strategy. Unlike high-growth sectors like blockchain or mining, metals provide stability and security for long-term investments.

    We consider metals the best way to protect capital, which is why we allocate a portion of profits from our more lucrative sectors to this area. For example, alongside a $5,000,000 investment in a new mining farm, we place an additional $5,500,000 in precious metals, securing long-term stability for our funds.

    Our approach balances risky and stable investments, sustaining business growth and ensuring positive financial results. Ignoring capital preservation by not investing in reliable assets risks financial instability.

    We are confident that, with our metals investment strategy (increasing by 10%), our business will remain stable and secure, unaffected by sector fluctuations.

  • 18%

    Buying back shares from insurance companies

    We buy back shares from companies looking to sell due to urgent financial needs, doubts about future prospects, or financial challenges. Partnering with insurance companies selling such shares provides us with unique profit opportunities.

    Our experts are skilled at identifying profitable deals, viewing these sales as a chance for high returns despite others’ uncertainties. These stocks often belong to new companies with uncertain futures, which presents both risks and opportunities.

    In certain cases, we achieve returns of 20% to 200%, though we carefully manage risk with stop orders at 18% to protect against market volatility. This approach enables us to safely purchase and quickly sell shares, focusing on short-term gains with managed risk, rather than long-term holding of these high-risk assets.

  • 3%

    Opening a franchised mining farm: co-operation and new opportunities

    We are open to cooperation and offer a unique opportunity to franchise a mining farm. Previously focused on hosting services, we now invest in mining farm creation alongside our partners, providing full technical support to ensure stability and profitability.

    In this partnership, we jointly earn from mining, with hosting fees paid separately for transparent payments and a clear distinction between mining and hosting profits.

    Our technical team maintains each partner farm, minimizing risks and maximizing efficiency. We anticipate hosting services will generate up to 3% of revenue, adding a stable income source.

    We are confident our franchise model will drive business growth and stable profits for all involved.
